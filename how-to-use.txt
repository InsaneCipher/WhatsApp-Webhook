How to use: 
To use the chat-bot simply enter a career orientated question and our bot will reply in a matter of minutes. Do not enter personal information such as name, date of birth, email or phone number please.

Example questions: 
"How do i get a internship in the UK?", 
"How can I improve my confidence for an interview?", 
"What are my options for studying abroad if I live in ______?"

BE WARNED ANY INAPPROPRATE MESSAGES OR QUESTIONS WILL BE LOGGED AND REPORTED!!
